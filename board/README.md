# Board of Directors

This directory contains Board of Directors meeting records, resolutions, and election documentation.

## Structure

- `meetings/` - Meeting minutes and records
- `resolutions/` - Board resolutions
- `elections/` - Director election records

## Meeting Minutes

Meeting minutes are organized by date and include:
- Attendance
- Agenda items
- Discussions
- Decisions and votes
- Action items

## Resolutions

Board resolutions are numbered and dated, including:
- Resolution number
- Title
- Motion text
- Vote results
- Effective date
